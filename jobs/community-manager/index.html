Community Manager

You are the type of person who loves to motivate and encourage others. You are passionate about technology, and love staying up-to-date on the latest developments. You are able to blend in and collaborate with diverse groups, whether it’s technical people or salespeople in suits. You are detail-oriented and love getting things done.

As the OpenZeppelin & zeppelinOS Community Manager, you will be the organizing brains and the welcoming heart of our global community. Specifically, you will:

  • Grow and energize the worldwide community of OpenZeppelin & zeppelinOS advocates.

  • Motivate leaders in the community to contribute code, content, evangelize and act as references.

  • Partner with the rest of the marketing organization to make community an extension of our marketing team through contributed content, references, and more.

  • Track metrics around the success of the OpenZeppelin & zeppelinOS community, including overall community participation and downstream impact on our business.

Requirements

  • A college degree or equivalent and at least 2 years of work experience.

  • History of participation in community organization.

  • Strong organizational and relationship-building skills.

  • Excellent writing skills.

  • Extroverted personality - you should get energy from interacting with others.

Apply

Send an email to jobs@zeppelin.solutions with the phrase "Community Manager" on the subject line.
