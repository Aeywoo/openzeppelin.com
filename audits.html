Join Zeppelin

Help us build the foundations of the decentralized web

Zeppelin is looking for skillful developers and professionals who are committed to facilitating the development and operation of decentralized applications.
Whether you’re passionate about the crypto space or cherish the idea of getting paid for doing meaningful open source work, we want to talk to you.

What we do

We build key infrastructure and perform security audits for smart contract systems that power the open economy of blockchain-based digital assets.

We care deeply about economic liberty, equality of opportunities and freedom to innovate. We build technology that gives individuals the leverage to exercise these rights in the face of any government, corporation or institution.

/ OUR VALUES

Community oriented

We prioritize building open source tools that advance the state of the art so more people can build on public blockchains.

Constant learning

We are driven by curiosity. You’re encouraged to experiment with the latest technologies and cryptanalysis.

Transparency

We give each other honest feedback to enable everyone to do their best work.

How we work

We’re focused on creating a collaborative work environment where people are happy and productive.

Zeppelin is remote by default

Our team members are distributed all over the world. We apply remote best practices so you’ll always be in sync with your team.

Flexible schedules

We focus on achievements, not working hours. Find the rhythm that works best for you.

Flexible paid time off

We believe in the importance of breaks to lead a balanced life. You decide when to take your vacations.

Buenos Aires Castle

You’re welcome to work from our very own Crypto Castle whenever you want. We’re pet friendly and have green spaces, healthy food, football, and yoga classes.
